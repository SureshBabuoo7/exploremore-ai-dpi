# Relationship Types

## Purpose

This document defines relationship types used within the ExploreMore AI Tourism Knowledge Graph.

---

## SAME_CIRCUIT

Meaning:

Two entities belong to the same tourism circuit.

Example:

Thiruchendur Murugan Temple
↔
Manapad Beach

---

## NEARBY_ATTRACTION

Meaning:

Two attractions are geographically close enough to be commonly visited together.

Example:

Our Lady of Snows Basilica
↔
Roche Park

---

## LOCATED_IN

Meaning:

Entity is located within a district, city or administrative area.

Example:

Thiruchendur Murugan Temple
→
Thoothukudi District

---

## CONNECTED_BY_ROAD

Meaning:

Two destinations are connected through a primary travel route.

---

## PILGRIMAGE_ROUTE

Meaning:

Two religious destinations are commonly visited as part of a pilgrimage journey.

---

## HERITAGE_CLUSTER

Meaning:

Two heritage assets belong to the same cultural or historical cluster.

---

## ECO_TOURISM_CLUSTER

Meaning:

Natural attractions belonging to the same ecological tourism zone.

---

## GOVERNANCE

Relationship types may evolve as the Knowledge Graph expands.

All new relationship types must be documented before use.
