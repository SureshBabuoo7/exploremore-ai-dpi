# Tourism Knowledge Graph Relationships

## Core Relationships

### Located In

Connects entities to districts.

### Near

Connects geographically nearby entities.

### Part Of

Connects entities to tourism circuits.

### Connected To

Connects transportation and tourism assets.

### Recommended With

Connects attractions frequently visited together.

### Hosts

Connects venues to events.

### Operated By

Connects tourism assets to organizations.
