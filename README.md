# ExploreMore AI

## Tourism Intelligence Infrastructure for Emerging Destinations

ExploreMore AI is an open research and development initiative exploring how Tourism Knowledge Graphs, AI-assisted travel planning, and open tourism data can improve destination discovery, local economic participation, and tourism management.

---

## Why ExploreMore AI Exists

Tourism information is fragmented across websites, maps, government portals, social media, travel platforms, and local knowledge sources.

This fragmentation creates challenges for:

* Tourists
* Local communities
* MSMEs
* Destination managers
* Government agencies

ExploreMore AI investigates how structured tourism intelligence systems can improve tourism experiences while supporting sustainable local economic development.

## Current Scope

### Tourism Knowledge Graph

Building a structured tourism dataset including:

* Attractions
* Heritage sites
* Religious destinations
* MSMEs
* Transport nodes
* Events
* Tourism circuits

### Tourism Circuits

Development of curated tourism circuits connecting destinations, experiences, and local businesses.

### AI Travel Planning

Prototype AI-assisted itinerary generation powered by tourism knowledge graph data.
